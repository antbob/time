O'Time

Live calendar app built exclusively for Apple Watch

Live visualisation of calendar events with week days availability and around the clock views so you know exactly where you are in your schedule at any given time. Built-in complications and notifications to keep track of ongoing events outside the app.

Why O'Time?

O'Time is relative or rather is a relative of a calendar and a timer.

Event Timers

Calendar events are timers that show the time remaining before any event starts and ends.

Day Progress

Calendar days are progress rings that show the overall availability of every day of the week.

Round Calendar

Day events are visually mapped on a virtual clock face to show an intuitive day schedule view.

Active Details

Call event phone number or navigate to event location or show event time in its timezone.

Features

It's easy if you try

Today View

As its name suggests only today's events are shown in this view. Use Watch digital crown to navigate between events.

Event Timers

Ongoing events are shown as timers with time left before event end. Future events display their start and end time.

Time Colors

Green is 1 hour or more, Yellow is 45 to 30 minutes, Orange is 30 to 15 minutes, Red is 15 minutes or less, Blue is free time.

Event Focus

Tap selected event to see additional details. For multiple ongoing events Complications track the event selected in this view.

Week View

Week view shows calendar days one week ahead from today. Use Watch digital crown to navigate between calendar days.

Day Stats

Shows how busy the day is as well as the number of calendar events that day. Day duration can be changed in settings.

Busy Colors

Blue is a free day with no events, Green to Yellow to Orange to Red progression from available to busy, tied to day duration.

Day Schedule

Tap selected calendar day to display that day schedule hour by hour and minute by minute, visually mapped to a virtual clock.

Clock View

Calendar events on a virtual clock. The day is split into AM view from 00:00 to 12:00 and PM view from 12:00 to 00:00.

Go Round

Use Watch digital crown to navigate between events. The clock will switch between AM and PM views as you go.

Duration Colors

Blue is free time, Red is 15 minutes or less, Orange is 15 to 30 minutes, Yellow is 30 to 45 minutes, Green is 1 hour or more.

Event Details

Selected event is highlighted and its start and end times are shown. Tap selected event to see additional details.

Privacy

Your data is yours and it stays on your wrist, it is that simple. O'Time does no data collection and will never violate your trust.
